# AI Delegation

[Assumption] In this vault, AI delegation means deciding what work to assign to an AI assistant, how to specify success, and how to review the result.

Delegation includes:

- Defining the task.
- Giving relevant context.
- Setting constraints.
- Choosing the right model or tool.
- Reviewing the output.
- Iterating when the result is not good enough.
