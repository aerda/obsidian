# AI Evaluation

[Assumption] In this vault, AI evaluation means judging whether an AI response is accurate, useful, complete, and appropriate for the task.

Evaluation should consider:

- Accuracy.
- Relevance.
- Completeness.
- Reasoning quality.
- Source quality when factual claims matter.
- Fit with the requested format or constraints.
