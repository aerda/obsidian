# How should I evaluate whether an AI response is good enough?

Related source: [[Effective Prompting Fundamentals]]

Evaluation criteria to develop:

- Is the answer accurate?
- Does it answer the real question?
- Does it match the requested format?
- Are assumptions labeled?
- Are uncertain claims marked clearly?
- Does the answer need sources?
- What would failure look like in the real world?
