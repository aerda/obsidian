# Effective prompts reduce ambiguity with context

[Certain] A prompt becomes more useful when it gives the AI enough context to infer the right scope, audience, depth, and purpose.

A vague request forces the AI to guess what matters. A contextual request explains what the user wants, why they want it, how the result will be used, and what the user already knows.

Useful context includes:

- The task.
- The audience.
- The user's background.
- The intended use.
- The desired depth.
- Relevant constraints such as region, time period, format, or domain.

This connects to [[Prompt Engineering]] because prompting is not only about phrasing a question. It is about reducing avoidable ambiguity.
