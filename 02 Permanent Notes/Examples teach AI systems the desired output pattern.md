# Examples teach AI systems the desired output pattern

[Certain] Examples help AI systems imitate a desired pattern when the output style is easier to show than describe.

This is especially useful for tone, transformation tasks, formatting, rewriting, classification, and style matching.

In technical language, this overlaps with [[Few-shot Prompting]]. In practical terms, it means: show the AI what "good" looks like.

Good examples should cover the range of cases the AI is likely to encounter. If examples are too narrow, the AI may imitate the surface pattern but fail on edge cases.
