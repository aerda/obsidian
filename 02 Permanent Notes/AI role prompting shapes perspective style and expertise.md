# AI role prompting shapes perspective style and expertise

[Certain] Specifying a role changes how an AI assistant frames, prioritizes, and communicates an answer.

Role prompting can define expertise, audience, communication style, or evaluation lens.

Examples:

- "Act as a UX design expert."
- "Explain this as an experienced science teacher speaking to a bright 10-year-old."
- "Review this as a skeptical technical advisor."

Role prompting is useful for feedback, brainstorming, critique, explanation, and domain-specific review.

Risk: a role can create false authority. The AI may sound expert even when the underlying answer is weak. For high-stakes claims, role prompting should be paired with source checking or explicit uncertainty.
